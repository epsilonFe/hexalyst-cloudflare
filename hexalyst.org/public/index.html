Mission

Hexalyst.org is dedicated to building resilience in cybersecurity through practical labs, community contributions, and transparent technical documentation.

Name

The name Hexalyst blends hexaferrum — the iron hexagon symbolizing resilience — with analyst, representing clarity and disciplined reasoning in cybersecurity.

Community

We contribute to the cybersecurity community by documenting real‑world labs, sharing best practices, and fostering collaboration grounded in operational reality.

Financial Freedom to Help Others
Repaying a student loan so I can give more time and support to my congregation and community.

After graduating, I found myself with a student loan to repay. My employer helped with part of the cost, but the rest was up to me. I worked hard to finish school and reach my goals, but now I am facing the challenge of paying off the remaining balance. This financial burden has made it difficult to fully focus on my passions and give back to my community.

Repaying my student loan will give me the freedom to help others through their spiritual needs and support my congregation, where I find guidance and peace. I believe that financial freedom will allow me to dedicate more time and energy to helping others, sharing wisdom from the scriptures, and encouraging those around me to pursue their own goals.

I hope my journey inspires others to follow their ideas, commit to them, and achieve their dreams. If you feel moved to support, share, or offer words of encouragement, your kindness will make a real difference. Thank you for considering my story and being part of this journey.
